# Requirements Document

## Introduction

The Vighneshwara Enterprises Vehicle Income Dashboard is a web-based application designed to help the scrap business track, monitor, and analyze their vehicle-related income and expenses. The company operates two vehicles (lorry and truck/auto) for scrap collection and transportation, and needs to track vehicle earnings, rental income, and operational expenses separately from material costs.

## Glossary

- **Dashboard**: The main web interface displaying vehicle income and expense data
- **Vehicle**: Company-owned transportation asset (lorry or truck/auto)
- **Vehicle_Income**: Revenue generated by a vehicle (rental fees, transportation charges)
- **Vehicle_Expense**: Operational costs for a vehicle (petrol, maintenance, repairs)
- **Rental_Income**: Money earned when vehicles are rented out for freelance work
- **Transportation_Income**: Money earned for scrap collection/delivery services
- **Daily_Vehicle_Report**: Summary of income and expenses for each vehicle per day
- **Net_Vehicle_Profit**: Vehicle income minus vehicle expenses for a given period
- **Scrap_Purchase**: Money spent buying scrap materials from suppliers
- **Scrap_Sale**: Money earned selling scrap materials to buyers
- **Scrap_Transaction**: A record of buying or selling scrap with quantity, rate, and total amount
- **Daily_Scrap_Profit**: Scrap sales minus scrap purchases for a given day
- **Total_Daily_Profit**: Combined profit from vehicle operations and scrap trading
- **Purchase_Bill**: A printable document showing scrap purchase details with supplier information
- **Sale_Bill**: A printable document showing scrap sale details with buyer information
- **Bill_Template**: The format and layout used for generating purchase and sale bills
- **Bill_Number**: Unique identifier assigned to each generated bill for tracking
- **Purchase_Voucher**: Local purchase voucher format matching existing offline bills
- **Scrap_Type**: Specific categories of scrap materials (K.B. WASTE, O.T.B., N.B., W.R.D., H.H.T., G.T.B., B.B., ROAD WASTE PLASTIC, DUPLEX WASTE, PLASTIC, BLOCK MATERIAL, WASTE PLASTIC)
- **HSN_Code**: Harmonized System of Nomenclature code for each scrap type
- **Tax_Invoice**: GST compliant tax invoice format for sales with GSTIN, state codes, and tax calculations
- **Reverse_Charge**: GST reverse charge mechanism indicator (Y/N)
- **Transportation_Mode**: Method of goods transportation
- **UOM_CMT**: Unit of Measurement Comment for products/services
- **Company_Logo**: The VES logo with red, green, and blue styling to be displayed on dashboard and bills

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Vehicle Income Tracking

**User Story:** As a business owner, I want to record vehicle income from rentals and transportation services, so that I can track how much money each vehicle generates.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a vehicle earns rental income (e.g., ₹18,000 for lorry rental), THE Dashboard SHALL record the income against the specific vehicle
2. WHEN a vehicle provides transportation services, THE Dashboard SHALL record the transportation fees earned
3. WHEN recording vehicle income, THE Dashboard SHALL require vehicle type (lorry or truck/auto), amount, date, and income type
4. THE Dashboard SHALL support different income types: rental income and transportation income
5. WHEN vehicle income is recorded, THE Dashboard SHALL update the daily vehicle earnings immediately

### Requirement 2: Vehicle Expense Management

**User Story:** As a business manager, I want to track vehicle expenses like petrol, maintenance, and repairs, so that I can calculate the actual profit from each vehicle.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN vehicle expenses occur, THE Dashboard SHALL record petrol costs, maintenance costs, and repair costs separately
2. WHEN recording expenses, THE Dashboard SHALL require vehicle identification, expense type, amount, date, and description
3. THE Dashboard SHALL support expense categories: petrol, maintenance, repairs, and other operational costs
4. WHEN expenses are recorded, THE Dashboard SHALL update the daily vehicle expense totals automatically
5. THE Dashboard SHALL prevent recording of material costs (scrap purchase/sale) in vehicle expenses

### Requirement 3: Daily Vehicle Profit Calculation

**User Story:** As a business owner, I want to see daily profit/loss for each vehicle, so that I can understand which vehicle is more profitable.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN viewing the dashboard, THE Dashboard SHALL display daily net profit (income - expenses) for each vehicle
2. WHEN vehicle income or expenses are updated, THE Dashboard SHALL recalculate profit margins automatically
3. THE Dashboard SHALL show separate profit calculations for lorry and truck/auto
4. WHEN a vehicle has only expenses or only income for a day, THE Dashboard SHALL still calculate and display the net result
5. THE Dashboard SHALL highlight profitable days in green and loss days in red

### Requirement 4: Vehicle Performance Analytics

**User Story:** As a business analyst, I want to compare vehicle performance over time, so that I can make decisions about vehicle utilization and maintenance.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN viewing analytics, THE Dashboard SHALL show weekly and monthly profit trends for each vehicle
2. THE Dashboard SHALL display which vehicle generates more rental income vs transportation income
3. WHEN comparing vehicles, THE Dashboard SHALL show expense breakdowns (petrol vs maintenance vs repairs)
4. THE Dashboard SHALL calculate average daily profit for each vehicle over selected periods
5. THE Dashboard SHALL identify the most profitable days and expense patterns for each vehicle

### Requirement 5: Vehicle Data Validation

**User Story:** As a system administrator, I want to ensure accurate vehicle data entry, so that financial calculations remain correct.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN recording vehicle income, THE Dashboard SHALL validate that amounts are positive numbers
2. WHEN selecting vehicles, THE Dashboard SHALL only allow selection of existing vehicles (lorry, truck/auto)
3. THE Dashboard SHALL prevent recording the same rental income twice for the same vehicle on the same date
4. WHEN recording expenses, THE Dashboard SHALL require expense category selection from predefined list
5. THE Dashboard SHALL validate that dates are not in the future for income/expense entries

### Requirement 6: Vehicle Dashboard Interface

**User Story:** As a daily user, I want a clear vehicle-focused interface, so that I can quickly record and view vehicle-related financial data.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the dashboard loads, THE Dashboard SHALL display both vehicles with their current day status
2. THE Dashboard SHALL provide quick-entry forms for vehicle income and expenses
3. WHEN switching between vehicles, THE Dashboard SHALL maintain separate views for lorry and truck/auto data
4. THE Dashboard SHALL display vehicle information in cards or sections for easy comparison
5. THE Dashboard SHALL provide mobile-friendly interface for field data entry

### Requirement 7: Historical Vehicle Records

**User Story:** As a business owner, I want to view historical vehicle performance, so that I can analyze long-term profitability trends.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN selecting date ranges, THE Dashboard SHALL show historical income and expense data for each vehicle
2. THE Dashboard SHALL provide monthly and yearly summaries for vehicle performance
3. WHEN viewing historical data, THE Dashboard SHALL show cumulative profits and total expenses per vehicle
4. THE Dashboard SHALL support filtering by vehicle type and date range
5. THE Dashboard SHALL export vehicle performance reports for accounting purposes

### Requirement 8: Scrap Purchase Management

**User Story:** As a scrap buyer, I want to record scrap purchases with quantities and rates, so that I can track how much money is spent on buying scrap materials.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN purchasing scrap, THE Dashboard SHALL record supplier name, scrap type from predefined list, quantity, rate per unit, and total amount
2. THE Dashboard SHALL support all scrap types from existing voucher: K.B. WASTE, O.T.B., N.B., W.R.D., H.H.T.(ONP), G.T.B., B.B., ROAD WASTE PLASTIC, DUPLEX WASTE, PLASTIC, BLOCK MATERIAL, WASTE PLASTIC
3. WHEN recording scrap purchases, THE Dashboard SHALL calculate total amount automatically (quantity × rate)
4. THE Dashboard SHALL allow recording HSN codes for each scrap type for tax compliance
5. WHEN scrap is purchased, THE Dashboard SHALL update the daily scrap purchase total and link to vehicle if applicable

### Requirement 9: Scrap Sale Management

**User Story:** As a scrap seller, I want to record scrap sales with quantities and rates, so that I can track revenue from selling scrap materials.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN selling scrap, THE Dashboard SHALL record buyer name, scrap type, quantity, rate per unit, and total amount
2. WHEN recording scrap sales, THE Dashboard SHALL calculate total amount automatically (quantity × rate)
3. THE Dashboard SHALL support the same scrap types used for purchases for consistency
4. WHEN scrap is sold, THE Dashboard SHALL update the daily scrap sale total
5. THE Dashboard SHALL allow recording multiple sales transactions per day

### Requirement 10: Daily Scrap Profit Calculation

**User Story:** As a business owner, I want to see daily scrap trading profit, so that I can understand the profitability of scrap operations separate from vehicle operations.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN viewing daily summary, THE Dashboard SHALL calculate scrap profit (total sales - total purchases)
2. THE Dashboard SHALL display scrap purchases, scrap sales, and net scrap profit separately
3. WHEN scrap transactions are updated, THE Dashboard SHALL recalculate scrap profit automatically
4. THE Dashboard SHALL show scrap profit trends over weekly and monthly periods
5. THE Dashboard SHALL highlight profitable scrap trading days vs loss days

### Requirement 11: Combined Business Profit Overview

**User Story:** As a business owner, I want to see total daily profit combining both vehicle operations and scrap trading, so that I can understand overall business performance.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN viewing the main dashboard, THE Dashboard SHALL display total daily profit (vehicle profit + scrap profit)
2. THE Dashboard SHALL show breakdown of profit sources: vehicle operations vs scrap trading
3. WHEN any income or expense is recorded, THE Dashboard SHALL update the total business profit automatically
4. THE Dashboard SHALL provide weekly and monthly views of combined business performance
5. THE Dashboard SHALL identify which business area (vehicles or scrap) contributes more to overall profit

### Requirement 12: Purchase Bill Generation

**User Story:** As a scrap buyer, I want to generate and print purchase bills matching our existing voucher format, so that I can maintain consistent documentation.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN generating a purchase voucher, THE Dashboard SHALL replicate the existing "LOCAL PURCHASE VOUCHER" format exactly
2. THE Dashboard SHALL include company header: "VIGHNESHWARA ENTERPRISES", Prop: Prasad Kode, complete address, GSTIN, VAT TIN, and phone number
3. WHEN creating purchase vouchers, THE Dashboard SHALL include voucher number, date, supplier name (Ms.), and vehicle number
4. THE Dashboard SHALL display scrap items in tabular format with columns: S.No., Particulars, HSN Code, Qty., Rate, Amount (Rs. Ps.)
5. THE Dashboard SHALL auto-populate scrap types from the predefined list and allow HSN code entry for each item

### Requirement 13: Sale Bill Generation

**User Story:** As a scrap seller, I want to generate GST compliant tax invoices matching our existing format, so that I can provide proper invoices to buyers.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN generating a sale invoice, THE Dashboard SHALL create a "TAX INVOICE" matching the existing format exactly
2. THE Dashboard SHALL include all company details, GSTIN (37CMJPK9992A1ZL), VAT TIN (37584920387), and bank details (HDFC Bank A/c No: 50200032392080, IFSC: HDFC0004802)
3. WHEN creating tax invoices, THE Dashboard SHALL include invoice number, date, reverse charge (Y/N), transportation mode, vehicle number, date of supply, and place of supply
4. THE Dashboard SHALL capture buyer details: Name, Address, GSTIN, State with state code for both "Billed to" and "Shipped to" sections
5. THE Dashboard SHALL calculate GST amounts: Total Amount Before Tax, Add SGST, Add CGST, Add IGST, Tax Amount GST, Total Amount After Tax

### Requirement 14: Bill Management and History

**User Story:** As a business administrator, I want to manage and track all generated bills, so that I can maintain proper business documentation.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Dashboard SHALL maintain a history of all generated purchase and sale bills
2. WHEN viewing bill history, THE Dashboard SHALL show bill numbers, dates, amounts, and parties involved
3. THE Dashboard SHALL allow reprinting of previously generated bills
4. WHEN searching bills, THE Dashboard SHALL support filtering by date range, bill type, and party name
5. THE Dashboard SHALL provide bill templates that can be customized with company logo and details

### Requirement 15: Bill Format and Content

**User Story:** As a business owner, I want bills that exactly match our existing offline formats with our company logo, so that our online system maintains consistency with current business practices.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN generating purchase vouchers, THE Dashboard SHALL use the exact layout, fonts, and spacing of the existing "LOCAL PURCHASE VOUCHER"
2. WHEN generating sale invoices, THE Dashboard SHALL replicate the "TAX INVOICE" format with proper GST compliance fields
3. THE Dashboard SHALL include the company logo (VES with red, green, blue styling) in the header of all bills and dashboard
4. THE Dashboard SHALL include bill value in words, common seal area, and authorized signature section on tax invoices
5. THE Dashboard SHALL support terms and conditions text and legal disclaimers as shown in existing formats and ensure all bills are print-ready with proper margins

### Requirement 16: Dashboard Branding and Logo

**User Story:** As a business owner, I want the dashboard to display our company branding, so that the system reflects our professional identity.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the dashboard loads, THE Dashboard SHALL display the Vighneshwara Enterprises logo prominently in the header
2. THE Dashboard SHALL use the company color scheme (red, green, blue) from the logo in the interface design
3. THE Dashboard SHALL display "VIGHNESHWARA ENTERPRISES" as the main title with proper styling
4. THE Dashboard SHALL include company contact information (Plot No. 25, Phase-2, Nellore address, phone: 9032164165) in the footer
5. THE Dashboard SHALL maintain consistent branding across all pages and bill generation screens
